Who we are ?

There are two main types of association: the "simple" association, not declared in the prefecture, has a legal existence, but cannot own assets or sue; the association declared in the prefecture has legal personality. She can therefore own assets and take legal action. Some of them have the special status of associations recognized as being of public utility by decree of the Council of State. Their purpose is deemed to be of general interest (e.g. the fight against certain diseases). This recognition allows them to receive donations and legacies, but in return they must present serious guarantees and are subject to stricter administrative control, in particular by the Court of Auditors.

We are the second one and this means we can guarantee you we will threat your childrens like ours.
